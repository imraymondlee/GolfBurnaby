Facilities

Riverway

The Riverway Golf Course, located off of Marine Drive in South Burnaby and less than ten miles east of the Vancouver airport. An array of white tan bunkers and water hazardss are strategically placed to make every shot exciting!

Burnaby Mountain

Burnaby Mountain Golf Course has been rated by Golf Digest as "One of the Best Places to Play" in North America! This popular golf course boasts 5,800 - 6,400 yards of natural tree-lined beauty and gentle rolling terrain which offers a blend of charm, character and serenity.

Kensington

Kensington is open year-round! Our Pitch and Putts provide a great, inexpensive, golfing experience for players of all calibres aged eight and up.

Central Park

Central Park Pitch and Putt provides a great golfing experience for players of all calibres aged eight and up. If you're a beginner, or just don't have time for a round on a full course, come enjoy a real golf experience within a beautiful wooded park setting.
