Our Golfing Lessons

Burnaby Golf has a long standing reputation for developing and teaching the game of golf in local schools and promoting interest for junior, beginner, and novice players through our wide variety of programs. We pride ourselves in offering golf courses with outstanding playing conditions year-round which offer challenges and enjoyment to golfers of all abilities at value driven rates.

Our Clubhouse

Riverway Clubhouse is a hidden gem located in the heart of Burnaby, serving outstanding food for exceptional value. Located on Riverway Golf Course; the Clubhouse offers beautiful wood beam architecture paired with floor-to-ceiling window views of the picturesque course - making for a dining experience you won't soon forget!

Our Philosophy to Learning

At any age or level, we promise to make golf more enjoyable for you! It is our goal at Burnaby Golf to make the game as easy as possible for you to learn, to enjoy, and to play for a lifetime.
