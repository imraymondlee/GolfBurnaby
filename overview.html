Overview

Burnaby Golf Facilities

The City of Burnaby operates four high-quality golf facilities which are professionally maintained year-round for your recreational enjoyment. Burnaby's natural beauty and spectacular public golf facilities offer many of the luxuries of a private club right in the heart of the lower mainland. We pride ourselves in offering golf courses with unique designs and outstanding year-round playing conditions that offer the challenges and enjoyment for golfers of all ages and abilities to enjoy within a competitive, fair price structure.

Enjoy the peaceful park-like settings of our two exceptional 18 hole golf courses - Riverway and Burnaby Mountain - and our two challenging pitch and putts (Kensington and Central Park). We also have two industry-leading Driving Ranges / Learning & Improvement Centers. Burnaby Golf has a long standing reputation for developing and teaching the game in local schools and driving interest for youth and new players through our wide variety of programs.

Our Goal for You

It is our goal at Burnaby Golf to make the game as easy as possible for you to learn, enjoy, and play for a lifetime! Our golf courses offer your group and or corporate clients the perfect atmosphere for the alternative meeting venue at very competitive rates.

Our Philosophy to Learning

Burnaby's golf development programs offer some of the finest and most comprehensive instruction available. The goal of our friendly, knowledgeable C.P.G.A professionals is to ensure that all students, from those learning the basic fundamentals to those committed to long-term personal improvement, can maximize their own golfing potential. Golf development programs are tailored to the needs of each individual. At any age or level, we promise to make golf more enjoyable for you! It is our goal at Burnaby Golf to make the game as easy as possible for you to learn, to enjoy, and to play for a lifetime. Our team of instructors are all members of the Canadian Professional Golfers Association and are dedicated to providing each and every student with an understanding of the fundamentals of golf within a relaxed and enjoyable learning environment!
