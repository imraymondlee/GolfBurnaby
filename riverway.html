About Riverway

The Riverway Golf Course, located off of Marine Drive in South Burnaby and less than ten miles east of the Vancouver airport, is a classic 18-hole championship golf course with challenging and diverse yardage of 5,400 - 7,000. Its imaginately designed links-style fairways are lined by sweeping mounds of wild fescure.

An array of white tan bunkers and water hazardss are strategically placed to make every shot exciting! Riverway is proud to be one of the premier championship golf courses in the greaterVancouver area and surrounding lower mainland. We sincerely hope your experience here will be both exciting and rewarding!
